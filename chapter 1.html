Japanese Curry

Japanese curry is one of Japan’s most popular comfort foods. It is a thick, mildly spiced, slightly sweet, and savory dish made with tender meat, potatoes, carrots, and onions simmered in a rich curry sauce and served over fluffy white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts (or beef)
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 pack Japanese curry roux
  • Cooked white rice (for serving)

Method

  1. Cut the chicken and vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pot over medium heat until lightly browned.
  3. Add the vegetables and stir for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 15–20 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  6. Turn off the heat and stir in the curry roux until fully dissolved.
  7. Simmer on low heat for another 5 minutes until thick.
  8. Serve hot over cooked white rice.
