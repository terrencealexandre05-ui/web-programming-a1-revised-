EUPHORIA

Created by Sam Levinson, Euphoria is a teen drama that follows a group of high school students as they navigate addiction, love, identity, friendship, trauma, social media, and relationships. The story is mainly told through the perspective of Rue Bennett, a teenager struggling with drug addiction after leaving rehab. The series is known for its striking visuals, emotional storytelling, and mature themes. (rotten tomatoes)

Characters

Main Cast & Characters

  • Zendaya as Rue Bennett – The narrator and central character, battling addiction while trying to find stability in her life.
  • Hunter Schafer as Jules Vaughn – A transgender student who forms a deep connection with Rue and struggles with identity and relationships.
  • Jacob Elordi as Nate Jacobs – A popular athlete with serious anger issues and a complicated personal life.
  • Sydney Sweeney as Cassie Howard – A teenager seeking love and validation while dealing with insecurity and emotional struggles.
  • Alexa Demie as Maddy Perez – Nate's confident and outspoken girlfriend with a strong personality.
  • Angus Cloud as Fezco – A drug dealer who becomes one of the most caring and supportive figures in Rue's life.
  • Maude Apatow as Lexi Howard – Cassie's quieter sister and one of Rue's closest friends.
  • Eric Dane as Cal Jacobs – Nate's father, whose hidden personal struggles affect his family.
